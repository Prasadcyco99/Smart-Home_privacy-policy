1 Information We Collect

When you interact with Smart Home through our website, enquiry forms, Facebook/Instagram lead ads, or WhatsApp, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal identification: Your name and phone number
  • Contact details: WhatsApp number or email address (if provided)
  • Enquiry details: The type of furniture you are interested in and your preferred showroom location
  • Communication history: Messages or notes shared with us via WhatsApp or phone calls
  • Device & usage data: Browser type, IP address, and pages visited on our website (collected automatically via cookies)

📌 We only collect information that is necessary to serve you better. We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, Aadhaar numbers, or passwords.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide for the following purposes:

  • To call or WhatsApp you back in response to your enquiry
  • To inform you about furniture products, offers, or promotions relevant to your interest
  • To schedule showroom visits or home delivery appointments
  • To improve our website experience and advertising campaigns
  • To send occasional updates about new collections or seasonal offers (you can opt out anytime)

We will never use your information for any purpose unrelated to Smart Home's products and services.

3 How We Share Your Information

We respect your privacy. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Meta (Facebook/Instagram): When you submit a lead form on our ads, your data is processed through Meta's platform per their privacy policy
  • With our staff: Your enquiry details are shared with our sales team only to follow up with you
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or government authority

✅ We do not share, rent, or sell your contact details to any marketing agencies, data brokers, or unrelated businesses.

4 Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience and track website performance. These may include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Meta Pixel to measure the performance of our Facebook and Instagram ads

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time, though some features of the website may not work correctly if you do so.

5 Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected:

  • Enquiry details are retained for up to 12 months from your last interaction with us
  • If you make a purchase, billing and order information may be kept for up to 7 years for accounting and legal compliance
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us

6 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to correction: Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Right to deletion: Request that we delete your personal data
  • Right to opt out: Unsubscribe from promotional messages at any time by replying "STOP" on WhatsApp or telling us over the phone

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 10 below.

7 Third-Party Links

Our website or social media pages may contain links to third-party websites such as Google Maps, WhatsApp, or Meta. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing your information with them.

8 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of our website or services after changes have been posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
